# Dashboard Example

```text
Use stitch-designer to generate three variants for an incident operations dashboard.

The dashboard is for on-call engineers. It should answer: what is broken, who owns it, what changed, and what action is next. Include severity filters, service status, recent deploys, open incidents, log snippets, and stale-data warnings. Keep it dense, calm, and readable. Avoid marketing-style cards and oversized hero sections.
```
